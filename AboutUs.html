J&J Autobody and Paint Customs is a Hollister local, family owned business that has been running for over two decades. It all started in a small garage with the help of a family friend named Ron Maywald. He has been a loyal part of the San Jose Roadster club for the past fifty years. Slowly but surely, the business grown to a size never imagined, composed out of hard working staff who are passionate about their work. At J&J, we break our backs day and night to leave a lasting influence on our customers, which we so dearly appreciate.


We are located on 4351 Pacheco Pass HWY in Hollister, California.
